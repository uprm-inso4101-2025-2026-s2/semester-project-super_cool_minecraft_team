What is the Minecraft Mod Analyzer?

The Minecraft Mod Analyzer is a web-based application designed to help players and modpack creators analyze the relationships between installed Minecraft mods.

By uploading a compressed modpack file, the system examines mod dependencies and detects potential compatibility conflicts between installed mods.

This tool simplifies the process of identifying problematic mods and helps users maintain stable and functional Minecraft modpacks.
